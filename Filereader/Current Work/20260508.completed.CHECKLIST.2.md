## Master/Detail Grid Design
- Optimize the top master grid around claim records and the lower detail grid around service-line records.
- Use a curated first-pass column set: `PrincipalMemberSurname`, `MembershipNumberProductCode`, `PatientName`, `AccountNumber`, `SchemeClaimNumber`, `TariffCode`, `ServiceDate`, `SchemePaymentIndicator`, `ClaimedAmount`, `AmountPaid`, `RejectionCode`, and `RejectionDescription`.
- Group detail records under expandable master claim rows using `SchemeClaimNumber`.
- Keep parsing and validation messages visible in the same display area.

## Explicit Non-Goals And Deferrals
- Do not implement `Medihelp Pay Instruction` parsing in this phase.
- Do not add `LineNo` or `RawLine`.
- Do not implement malformed-line recovery.
- Do not use `PAMC.Formats.Utilities.BuildRegistry()`.
- Do not add user-configurable columns.
- Do not add a separate display-mode toggle.
